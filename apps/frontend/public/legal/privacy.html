  • This Privacy Policy is a general notice of our data practices. If you have a subscription agreement, order form, or data processing addendum with HumanGrowth, that agreement controls to the extent it contains more specific terms for Customer Data.

At a Glance

How We Protect Your Personal Information

We have implemented the following measures to protect your personal information:

  • We limit collection of personal information to what is strictly necessary.
  • We use SSL encryption or other secure technologies when receiving or sending information beyond internal networks.
  • We destroy or delete personal information once no longer needed.
  • We conduct regular risk assessments.
  • We maintain comprehensive written security policies and procedures.
  • We train employees on security best practices.
  • We require employees to sign confidentiality agreements.
  • We encrypt laptops, USB devices, and other portable media.
  • We implement and monitor intrusion prevention and detection systems.
  • We maintain updated software and security safeguards.

No transmission or storage method is completely secure. If you believe your account or information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.
